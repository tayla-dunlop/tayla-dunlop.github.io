My Wonderful Learning Plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

My long-term goal is to complete this course and join the software developer workforce and eventually build my way up from there by gaining more experience.

A description of your strengths and limitations concerning learning.

In terms of learning, my main strength would be my motivation. I am a glass half full kind of person which I think greatly contributes to my motivation. Even when I’m stuck or learning something that I may not be that passionate about, I still seem to have a strong sense of motivation which helps me to get my work done in certain timeframes. My limitations for learning are myself. Although I feel good about my motivation, I can be extremely self-critical. Sometimes it will cause me to second guess myself and then I slow myself down.

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

Although I believe I can be a good communicator, it takes me a while to get comfortable with new people which means it takes me a while to start communicating well with a new person. During this course I would love to learn how to trust myself and be more comfortable with working with people who I don't know and being able to communicate well straight off the bat.
