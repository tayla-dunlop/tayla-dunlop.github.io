Research Neuroplasticity According to CNS (center for neuro skills), there are 10 important principles to remember about neuroplasticity: Use it or lose it , Use it and improve it , specificity , repetition matters , intensity matters , time matters , salience matters , age matters , generalization , and lastly Interference. As you can tell, all of these are extremely important with neuroplasticity and if you can practice these 10 principles while learning, you will be a happy genius in no time!

During this course, it is super important for me to practice those principles as I am learning so many new things in such a short time, it is important that I build super strong connections in my brain so I can put them to use as soon as possible. What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity? Same as the 10 principles above, practice, repeat, and so on!
